# Country_scope_Governments_in_Exile
# Source: https://hoi4.paradoxwikis.com/Effect

## add_legitimacy
- Params: Adds legitimacy to a government in exile.
- Example: add_legitimacy = 10
- Desc: Adds legitimacy.
- Since: 1.6

## set_legitimacy
- Params: Sets the legitimacy of governments in exile.
- Example: set_legitimacy = 10
- Desc: Sets legitimacy.
- Since: 1.6

## become_exiled_in
- Params: Makes a country a government in exile in a set country, with a set starting legitimacy.
- Example: become_exiled_in = { target = <Host tag> legitimacy = <0-100> (starting legitimacy, optional) }
- Desc: Creates a government in exile.
- Since: 1.6

## end_exile
- Params: Ends a government in exile.
- Example: end_exile = yes
- Desc: Ends a government in exile.
- Since: 1.6
